## Introduction

The development of AI technology has brought new possibilities to note management. This article shares how to use AI assistants to improve note-taking efficiency.

## AI Applications in Note-Taking

### 1. Smart Summarization

AI can quickly extract key points from articles:

- Auto-generate summaries
- Extract key information
- Structured organization

### 2. Content Expansion

Based on existing notes, AI can:

- Supplement related knowledge
- Provide different perspectives
- Generate examples

### 3. Knowledge Association

AI helps build knowledge networks:

- Discover related notes
- Build knowledge graphs
- Recommend related content

## Practical Tips

### Prompt Design

Good prompts are key:

```
Please summarize the core ideas of this article
and list 3 key takeaways.
```

### Iterative Optimization

Continuously improve your workflow:

1. Record use cases
2. Analyze effectiveness
3. Adjust strategy
4. Keep improving
